Library

A short list of books, essays, blogs, papers, and people I think are worth reading or following. This is not meant to be exhaustive, just a record of some things I’ve found useful.

Books

  • The Sovereign Individual — for its ambition, even where it overreaches.
  • The Revolt of the Public — useful for thinking about institutions and public life.
  • The Beginning of Infinity — one of the better books on progress and explanation.
  • Meditations — still good on discipline and perspective.

Essays and blogs

  • Paul Graham
  • Slate Star Codex / Astral Codex Ten
  • LessWrong
  • Tim Gowers
  • Dominic Cummings

Papers and ideas

  • Work on institutions, state capacity, and public administration.
  • Papers and posts on AI capability, reasoning, and software design.
  • Good writing on media, information flow, and how ideas spread.

People worth following

  • Paul Graham
  • Andrej Karpathy
  • Tyler Cowen
  • Patrick Collison
  • Steve Hsu

Why this page exists

A lot of what I think about comes from reading people who are clearer, stranger, or more ambitious than average. This page is just a small attempt to keep track of some of them.
