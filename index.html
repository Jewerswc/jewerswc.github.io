William Jewers

I build software for institutional settings and write about the way ideas move.

I’m the founder of ParlyReply, an AI-native CX platform for the public sector. Before that I studied philosophy and politics, worked in and around the UK House of Commons, and taught myself to code so I could design and ship products myself.

The recurring thread in my work is the same: institutions, AI, the distribution of ideas, and products that make complex systems clearer and easier to use.
