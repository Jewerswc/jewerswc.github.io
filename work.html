What I do

I'm building Interesting Or Not is the future of news: an LLM-powered news platform that replaces writers with LLM personas. It places readers 'in the loop' by adapting LLMs to reader feedback and engagement. Think of it like this: Youtube, but for writing where all the content is LLM generated and iterated by human inputs.

What I've done
